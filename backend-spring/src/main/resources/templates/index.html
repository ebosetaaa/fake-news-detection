Models & Capabilities
One model.
Deep context.
01
BERT Transformer
Fine-tuned bert-base-uncased with bidirectional context encoding. Produces a 768-dimensional [CLS] embedding capturing deep semantic patterns — achieving 96.8% accuracy on WELFake.
02
Social Signal Fusion
Shares, likes, and comments are encoded through a dedicated MLP branch and fused with the BERT embedding — adding distributional social context to the semantic analysis.
03
Hybrid Architecture
The 768-dim BERT embedding and 32-dim social MLP output are concatenated into an 800-dim fused representation, passed through a classification head for a unified prediction.
04
Explainability Layer
BERT attention weights highlight which tokens influenced the prediction. SHAP token attribution reveals the marginal contribution of each word — giving human-readable justification for every verdict.
05
Confidence Scoring
Every prediction returns a probability score from 0–100%, not just a binary label. Giving nuanced, actionable output for fact-checkers, journalists, and researchers.
06
Audit & Export
Every prediction is logged with a timestamp and content hash for accountability. Results can be exported as structured JSON for integration into downstream workflows.

Process
How it
works.
01
Submit your article
Paste the full text of any news article into the input form. The system accepts articles and headlines of any length.
02
Preprocessing pipeline
Text is cleaned and tokenized using HuggingFace BertTokenizer into WordPiece subword tokens, padded to 256 tokens.
03
BERT inference
Tokenized input and normalized social signals are passed through the BERT hybrid model — [CLS] embedding fused with social MLP output.
04
Verdict & explanation
The classification head outputs a REAL/FAKE prediction with confidence score, returned with BERT attention weights for explainability.
